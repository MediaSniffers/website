Who We Are

Since inception in 2013, our journey has been a roller coaster ride filled with a lot of unique experiences that helped us emerge as an amazing team that consists of superb organizers, exciting marketers, aesthetics-filled designers. Our team still learns like students and strives for better as we believe only a mediocre dies at its best.

Media Sniffers is a full-service experiential marketing and event management company providing cutting edge essentials in making your project unique and competitive. Working closely with the client, our team of experts adds a new experience to your event making it more impactful as well as profitable.

With exposure of working with high-end clients from corporate, diplomat and government sectors, we have gained excellence in managing activities of all sorts with no location barriers.
